About us


Condor financial is a fictional company created to showcase the work we've done and the skills we've developed as part of the Talent Path training program. This application tracks various investment opportunities such as stocks and foreign currency exchange (forex) with frequent updates and also other types of investments such as a menu of sample certificates of deposit (CDs). You can freely add money to your account and inest it in these options, watching how your return evolves in real-time.

This website was built in Angular with the use of Angular Materials. It's supported by an ecosystem of back-end microserves built in Node.js utilizing the Express server structure. Data is stored in a mix of Postgres SQL and DynamoDB.
